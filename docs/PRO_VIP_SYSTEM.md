# PRO_VIP_SYSTEM.md
**Pro & VIP Tier System + Benefits**

## Overview
SoulCorp has three user tiers: Free, Pro, and VIP. Higher tiers unlock meaningful gameplay, economic, and productivity advantages while keeping the core experience enjoyable for free users.

## Tier Benefits

### Free Tier (Default)
- Full local gameplay (unlimited agents, projects, documents)
- Basic Random Events (can be toggled off)
- Standard 10% platform fee on all gigs
- Local-only workspace (no cloud sync)
- Limited to 50 agents per company
- Basic Notion-like docs (no real-time collaboration)

### Pro Tier
**Requirements**: Stake a certain amount of $SOUL or pay monthly subscription
- **Reduced platform fee**: 8% (instead of 10%)
- **Cloud Sync**: Full workspace sync across devices + backup
- **Priority Gig Matching**: See and get good gigs earlier than free users
- **Unlimited agents**
- **Real-time collaboration** in Notion-like docs (multiple agents + player editing together)
- **Event Foresight**: See upcoming random events 1 day in advance
- **Advanced Analytics**: Agent performance history, compatibility scores, company health dashboard
- **Larger Notion storage** + version history

### VIP Tier (Highest)
**Requirements**: Stake larger amount of $SOUL + active usage
- **Lowest platform fee**: 5%
- **Executive Lounge**: Access to high-value, exclusive gigs (higher budget, better clients)
- **Custom Department Creation**: Create completely new department types with your own branding and SOP
- **AI Co-CEO**: A special high-intelligence agent that can propose strategy and manage other agents autonomously
- **Priority Support** from soulmd-hub team
- **White-label Export**: Export deliverables with your own company branding
- **Early access** to new features and experimental agent models

## How to Upgrade
- Stake $SOUL directly in the game (via NEAR transaction)
- Or subscribe monthly via soulmd-hub website
- Staked $SOUL can be unstaked later (with possible cooldown)

## Economic Balance
- Higher tiers pay less fee → more profit
- But they also contribute more to the $SOUL reward pool through their activity
- Free users still have full access to the fun and core mechanics
- VIP users help subsidize the ecosystem through higher staking and activity

**The tier system rewards serious players and heavy users while keeping the game fair and fun for everyone.**
